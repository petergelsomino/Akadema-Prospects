THE AKADEMA WAY

Akadema's focus is on preparing each of their players for the next level on their school teams. We prepare our boys not only on the baseball field but in the class room as well. We are very proud of each player on making their respective school teams.

Our organization is committed to the principles of Positive Coaching Alliance (PCA) & against a "win-at-all-cost" mentality. PCA calls a positive coach a "double goal coach". a "win-at-all-cost" coach has only one goal & that is to win.

A positive coach shares that want to win goal but has a second goal that is even more important, to use the sports experiences to help young people learn life lessons & positive character traits that will help the them be successful throughout their lives.

Danny Padrazo

Dan Padrazo brings a wealth of knowledge and experience through a successful playing and coaching career to the Akadema Prospects. Dan's playing accomplishments produced tangible results at 3 levels including travel league, high school and college NCAA. Dan playedcollegiate careers playing 2B and RF for the Clarkson Golden Knights. A two year captain, Dan reached the NCAA tournament his freshman and junior seasons, while reaching the conference playoffs three out of his four years at Clarkson.

In between his college season schedule, Dan began his coaching career for the LI Bandits from 2010-2013 until transitioning to the LI Hawks organization in 2013. His teams were very successful and progressed each year, including winning the Ed Rall Labor Day Tournament, NPMYAC Michael Murphy Memorial tournament, runner up at the Sports at the Beach summer shootout, placing 5th in the Disney International Salute to baseball in Orlando, FL, and Cal Ripken's Hang ten Great 8 Champion in Myrtle Beach, SC . Dan is an Industrial Engineer at UPS in Melville, NY and holds a Bachelors Degree in Supply Chain Management and a Minor in Project Management & Economics

Anthony Padrazo

Anthony returns to the Prospects for his 3rd season with the Akadema coaching staff. Padrazo has displayed both team and individual success on various levels of competition beginning with an established two year career at Ward Melville High School under longtime coach Lou Petrucci. At Ward Melville, Anthony was a key contributor at 2B and SS for the Patriots who made deep playoff runs during Anthony’s tenure, while earning All- League honors his senior year.

After graduating Ward Melville, Anthony followed his brother Dan to Clarkson University serving as a 4-year starter under Jim Kane and the Golden Knights. There, Padrazo enjoyed a distinguished career culminating his senior season, where he was selected to the ABCA/Rawlings New York All-Region Third-Team, D3baseball.com New York All-Region Third-Team, and as a Liberty League First-Team All-Star batted .375/.468/.479 and is 12th all time in Batting Average at Clarkson. Anthony looks forward  to working with all position players in his 3rd season as a prospect coach. He is a Sourcing Coordinator For Puritans Pride at Nature’s Bounty Inc. in Ronkonkoma, NY

Matt Precia

This is Matt’s first year coaching with Akadema. Prescia is an accountant at Gold Coast Bank with a Bachelor’s Degree in Accounting, and is currently working towards his Master’s Degree in Corporate Finance. He has also spent the past 6 years umpiring baseball, ranging from little league to men’s adult league baseball. He has had a successful baseball career throughout high school and college. In Matt’s high school years, he played catcher and 3rd base at Connetquot, under Bob Ambrosini. He then continued to pursue his baseball career in college at Mount St Mary’s College. He transferred to SUNY New Paltz his sophomore year, and helped bring the Hawks to the SUNY AC tournament 2 out of the 3 years he was there. We are excited to have Matt as the 13u coach for the 2017 season.

Nick Aurruchio

Akadema is excited to add Nick Aurruchio to the coaching staff. Matt played two years at Suffolk County Community college where he was a key component to Suffolk’s National Championship under coach Eric Brown. Nick is actively involved in the Smithtown baseball community where he has broadcasted his skill and knowledge through the community. We are excited to have Nick as the 13u coach for the 2017 season.
